About Us

Our Vision

At Bola Adebule and Co, we believe that anything is possible with hard work, dedication, and a commitment to excellence. Our vision of becoming a leading real estate company in Africa is just the beginning – we're excited to see what the future holds.

FACILITIES MANAGEMENT: At Bola Adebule and Co, we understand that your property portfolio is more than just a collection of assets – it's a key part of your business. That's why we take a proactive, results-driven approach to facilities management, helping you achieve your goals and maximize your ROI.

PROPERTY & ASSET VALUATION: Valuing your assets is more than just a numbers game – it's about understanding the market, your business objectives, and your unique circumstances. That's why we take a tailored approach to property and asset valuation, ensuring that you receive accurate, reliable results every time.

INVESTMENT & PROJECT MANAGEMENT: At Bola Adebule and Co, we understand that real estate investments require more than just financial capital – they also require expertise, experience, and a solid plan. That's why we offer comprehensive investment and project management services, designed to help you succeed in the competitive real estate market.

ESTATE AGENCY: Buying, selling, or renting a property can be a daunting process, but it doesn't have to be. Our estate agency department specializes in bringing buyers and sellers, landlords and tenants together, making the process smoother and more efficient.

Who we are: We are a firm of registered Estate surveyors and valuers with corporate Head office at No 34/36 Ikorodu road, 2nd Floor suite 2c beside Access Bank, Jibowu, Yaba Lagos. Our Firm is registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission CAC. We take pride in being a fast-growing key player in the real estate industry. Our team of registered Estate surveyors and valuers are committed to providing top-quality consultancy services to clients, helping them make informed decisions about their real estate investments.
